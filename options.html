Dual Timer Modes

Po-My-Doro has two ways to track your time. You can flip between them using the toggle at the bottom of the timer.

Normal Mode Classic Pomodoro

The standard way to work. It cycles automatically between focus periods and breaks.

  • Work Session (Default 25m): Put your head down and work.
  • Short Break (Default 5m): Take a quick breather.
  • Long Break (Default 15m): A longer rest after you finish 4 work sessions in a row.

Focused Mode Flow State Tracking

Sometimes you just get in the zone and don't want a timer interrupting you. Focused Mode drops the countdown.

  • Acts as a regular stopwatch counting up.
  • Keeps tracking until you actually click Stop or Pause.
  • Great for deep reading, unrestricted coding, or when you just want to see how long a task takes.

Audio Cues & Notifications

You don't need to keep checking the clock. Subtle sounds let you know what's happening:

  • Start Chime: A soft tick marks the beginning of your work block.
  • Pre-End Warning: (Normal Mode Only) A gentle chime warns you when your session is almost over. You can change this timing in the popup settings.
  • Completion Chime: A distinct sound lets you know the session is done.

Analytics & History

Look back at how you spent your time. All your history stays securely on your own computer.

  • Day View: A visual timeline of your day. Green blocks are work sessions. Normal and Focused tags show exactly how you worked.
  • Week & Month Views: See your total hours across longer periods to figure out your most productive days.
  • Real-time Totals: If you have a timer running right now, it counts toward your daily totals in real-time.

Configuration & Data Management

Your data belongs to you. Po-My-Doro saves everything straight to your browser—no cloud required.

  • In-Popup Settings: Click the settings icon in the top right of the popup to change your default durations and audio warnings.
  • Export & Import: Back up your entire history to a JSON file, or move it to another computer. Click your profile icon in the popup to open Data Management.
