# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Data Collection

Po-My-Doro does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage

All data (timer settings, session history) is stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. No data ever leaves your browser.

## Third-Party Services

Po-My-Doro does not connect to any external servers or third-party services.

## Data Export

The extension provides a JSON export/import feature for manual backup and device migration. This is entirely user-initiated and no data is sent to any server.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](../../issues).
